Privacy Policy for Consumed Extension

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

The Consumed extension collects minimal information to function properly:

  • Settings Data: Your hourly wage, display preferences, and customization settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as names, email addresses, or browsing history.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any other tracking mechanisms.

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Calculating work hours for prices on skroutz.gr
  • Displaying your preferred settings and customizations
  • Enabling the extension to function as intended

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API.
  • Chrome Sync: If you have Chrome sync enabled, your settings may be synced across your devices through Google's secure infrastructure.
  • No External Servers: We do not send any data to external servers or third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

5. Data Retention

Your settings are retained as long as you have the extension installed. When you uninstall the extension, all data is automatically removed from your device.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored settings through the extension's options page
  • Modify or delete your settings at any time
  • Uninstall the extension, which will remove all stored data

7. Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the version number in the Chrome Web Store.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing or create an issue on our GitHub repository.

10. Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) requirements
  • Chrome Extension Manifest V3 security standards
